Keyboard Cleaning

Turn off and unplug your computer. Use compressed air or a small brush to remove debris from the keyboard. Do not use liquids.

Fan Cleaning

Dust buildup in fans causes overheating. Use compressed air to gently blow dust out of the vents and internal fans without touching the blades.

Software Updates

Regularly check for and install operating system and driver updates. Updates improve performance and fix security vulnerabilities.

Antivirus Scan

Use trusted antivirus software to scan your system weekly. It helps remove malware and protects your data.
